INTRODUCTION

We are one of the biggest Christian youth organisations in Rivers State

The Boys' Brigade is the first Voluntary Christian Uniformed Youth Organisation in the world that trains Boys to become Disciplined, God-fearing, Self-Reliant and good citizens of their Communities. We have faith in young people and provide opportunities for children and young people to learn, grow and discover in a safe, fun and caring environment which is rooted in the Christian faith

OUR OBJECT:

The Object of The Boys' Brigade is: "The advancement of Christ's kingdom among Boys and the promotion of habits of Obedience, Reverence, Discipline, Self-respect and all that tends towards a true Christian manliness."

OUR MOTTO:

The Boys' Brigade Motto is: 'Sure & Steadfast' The motto is taken from the bible, the book of Hebrews, Chapter 6, Verse 19.
